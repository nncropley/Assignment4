About Us

Welcome to Todd's Retro Games, where nostalgia meets modern gaming passion! Founded in 2005 by Todd Davidson, a dedicated gamer and collector with a lifelong love for retro gaming, our store is a haven for enthusiasts seeking classic titles and vintage consoles. At Todd's Retro Games, we specialize in curating a diverse collection of retro video games and consoles from the 80s, 90s, and early 2000s. Our shelves are stocked with beloved classics, rare finds, and timeless favorites, catering to both seasoned collectors and newcomers eager to explore the roots of gaming history. Currently we only have one location which you can find listed at the bottom of the page, but we are looking to expland very soon with a second store so you can find all the retro games you need close to you!
